Our Education

  • Our Vision

    We strive to develop best practice in the provision of an early childhood service. We inspire a life-long love of learning to help our children become contributing members of the world.

  • Our Mission

    We focus on the whole child by providing an environment that allows children to learn by doing – moving from concrete, hands-on experiences to more abstract concept development.

  • We care

    A caring, cooperative school environment promotes cultural values and supports positive relationships and respectful interaction.
